About Perreo Por Una Causa

Perreo Por Una Causa is hosted by a group of Puerto Ricans living in the Bay Area who love to dance reggaeton and want to do some good in the world. But really, who doesn't?

We seek to create a space where people can come together through music, dancing, and a desire to support social justice causes. We guarantee this will be one of the funnest fundraisers you've ever been to!
